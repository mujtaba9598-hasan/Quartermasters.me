# Management Consultancies

## Scope & Description
Strategic advisory for organizational leadership, governance frameworks, and operational excellence. We help organizations scale, restructure, and navigate complex market dynamics with institutional-grade rigor.

### What We Do
- Corporate Governance Frameworks
- Operational Efficiency Audits & Process Optimization
- Change Management & Organizational Restructuring
- Market Entry Strategy & Competitive Positioning
- Executive Dashboarding & KPI Design

### What We Don't Do
- Interim CEO/CFO placement
- Legal counsel (we partner with firms)
- Financial auditing (external audit function)

## Service Tiers & Deliverables

### Express ($1,800)
- **Scope**: Strategic health check or business plan validation.
- **Deliverables**: SWOT Analysis, Strategic Gap Report, Basic Financial Model Review.
- **Timeline**: 2 Weeks.

### Standard ($30,000 - $55,000)
- **Scope**: Organizational restructuring or market entry strategy.
- **Deliverables**: Target Operating Model (TOM) Design, Go-to-Market Strategy, Governance Manual.
- **Timeline**: 3-4 Months.

### Premium ($75,000 - $140,000)
- **Scope**: Merger integration or full-scale digital transformation setup.
- **Deliverables**: Integration PMO Setup, Synergy Realization Plan, Change Management Roadmap.
- **Timeline**: 6-9 Months.

### Enterprise ($150,000+)
- **Scope**: Government-level strategic initiatives or multinational transformation programs.
- **Deliverables**: National Strategic Framework, Regulatory Roadmap, Sovereign Asset Strategy.
- **Timeline**: 12+ Months.

## Process Timeline
1. **Assess (Week 1-4)**: Data gathering, stakeholder interviews, and baseline assessment.
2. **Strategize (Week 5-8)**: Developing the future state blueprint and implementation plan.
3. **Align (Week 9-12)**: Board approval, resource allocation, and communication strategy.
4. **Execute (Week 13+)**: Deployment of initiatives and PMO oversight.

## FAQ

### 1. How do you measure success?
We define success metrics (KPIs) at the start of the engagement—typically ROI, cost reduction, or revenue growth—and track them rigorously.

### 2. Do you execute the strategy?
We provide the *roadmap* and *PMO support*. Execution is led by the client's management team with our oversight.

### 3. What industries do you specialize in?
We are sector-agnostic but have deep expertise in real estate, finance, and logistics.

### 4. Can you help with fundraising?
We help structure the *business case* and *pitch materials*, but we do not broker deals or raise capital directly.

### 5. How does your governance framework differ from standard templates?
Ours is tailored to the specific cultural and operational context of UAE family businesses and government entities.

### 6. Do you offer interim management?
No, we advise the existing management or help recruit new executives.

### 7. What is your methodology?
We use a blend of classical strategy frameworks (Porter, McKinsey) adapted for the agile, high-growth environment of the UAE.

### 8. How involved does the CEO need to be?
Critical involvement is required during the Diagnosis and Strategy formulation phases. Execution can be delegated to the COO/GM.

### 9. Can you help with digital transformation?
Yes, we design the *strategic roadmap* and partner with our Tech R&D arm for technical implementation.

### 10. Do you provide market research?
Yes, as part of our strategic formulation process, we conduct primary and secondary market research.
