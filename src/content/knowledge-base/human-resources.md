# Human Resources Consultancy

## Capital is useless without Execution
In the high-velocity markets of the UAE, capital availability is rarely the constraint. The true bottleneck is **Human Capital**. Quartermasters F.Z.C views Human Resources Consultancy not as administrative support, but as a core strategic function. We align your workforce with your commercial objectives, ensuring that every hire is an asset that appreciates in value.

Licensed under Activity 37357, our mandate covers the full spectrum of HR management, from the granular details of labor contracts to the high-level architecture of organizational charts.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Strategic Talent Acquisition
Recruitment in the UAE requires a nuanced understanding of global talent pools and local regulatory frameworks.
- **Executive Search**: Identify and secure C-suite leadership capable of navigating regional complexity.
- **Technical Deployment**: Sourcing specialized technical staff for project-critical roles.
- **Cultural Fit Assessment**: Ensuring candidates align with the ethos of your organization, reducing turnover.

### 2. Regulatory Compliance (MOHRE & Free Zones)
Navigating the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (MOHRE) and various Free Zone regulations is critical to operational continuity.
- **Contract Drafting**: Creating employment contracts that protect the employer's intellectual property and commercial interests while remaining compliant with UAE Labor Law.
- **Visa & Immigration Strategy**: Optimizing the allocation of visa quotas and managing the end-to-end residency process.
- **WPS Compliance**: ensuring full adherence to the Wage Protection System to avoid blockages and fines.

### 3. Organizational Development
We build the structures that allow teams to scale.
- **Performance Management Frameworks**: KPIs and OKRs that drive actual business results, not just box-ticking.
- **Compensation & Benefits Structuring**: Designing packages that attract top talent while maintaining fiscal discipline.
- **Succession Planning**: identifying and grooming the next generation of leadership within your organization.

## The Human Capital Lifecycle
Our methodology follows the employee journey from acquisition to offboarding.

### Phase 1: Architecture
We define exactly what the organization needs before a single job description is written.
- **Workload Analysis**: Determining the precise number of heads required to deliver the business plan.
- **Role Definition**: Creating rigorous job descriptions with clear accountability matrices.

### Phase 2: Acquisition & Onboarding
We manage the intake process to ensure swift integration.
- **Sourcing & Screening**: managing the funnel to present only the most qualified candidates.
- **Onboarding Programs**: Structured integration plans that reduce the "time-to-value" for new hires.

### Phase 3: Retention & Development
We implement systems to keep top talent engaged.
- **Learning & Development**: Training programs tailored to close skill gaps and prepare staff for promotion.
- **Employee Relations**: Managing grievances and disciplinary procedures in full compliance with the law.
