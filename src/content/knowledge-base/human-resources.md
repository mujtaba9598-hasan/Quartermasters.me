# Human Resources Consultancy

## Scope & Description
Strategic HR advisory focused on talent acquisition, workforce deployment, organizational development, and compliance navigation. We architect the human capital infrastructure required for high-performance organizations operating in the UAE.

### What We Do
- Workforce Planning & Organizational Design
- Executive Search & Talent Acquisition Strategy
- HR Policy Development & Labor Law Compliance
- Performance Management Systems
- Emiratization Strategy & Execution

### What We Don't Do
- Staffing agency services (blue-collar bulk recruitment)
- Payroll processing (we advise on structure, not execution)
- Legal representation in labor courts

## Process Timeline
1. **Diagnosis (Week 1-2)**: Cultural audit and capability assessment.
2. **Design (Week 3-6)**: Structuring the organizational chart and policy framework.
3. **Deployment (Week 7-12)**: Implementation of systems and hiring of key personnel.
4. **Optimization (Ongoing)**: KPI tracking and iterative refinement.

## Regulatory Context
- Regulated by: Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (MOHRE), Free Zone Authorities.
- Key Laws: UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021 (Labor Law).
- Compliance: Wage Protection System (WPS), Emiratization quotas (Nafis).

## Target Audience
- Pre-IPO startups scaling their teams.
- Family conglomerates professionalizing management.
- International firms establishing UAE regional HQs.

## FAQ

### 1. Do you provide recruitment services?
We provide the *strategy* and *architecture* for recruitment. For executive search, we partner with specialized headhunters under our strategic oversight.

### 2. Can you help with Emiratization?
Yes, we design comprehensive Emiratization strategies to meet government quotas while ensuring genuine value addition to the organization.

### 3. Do you write employee contracts?
We draft the *templates* and *policy clauses* in line with UAE law, which should then be reviewed by legal counsel.

### 4. What is the difference between HR consultancy and a recruitment agency?
An agency fills seats; we build the system that defines *what* seats are needed, *how* to fill them, and *how* to retain talent.

### 5. Can you audit our existing HR department?
Yes, our HR Health Check covers compliance, efficiency, and cultural alignment.

### 6. Do you handle visa processing?
We advise on the *process* and *structure* of visa quotas, but operational processing (PRO services) is a separate function.

### 7. How quickly can we set up an HR department?
A functional HR framework can be deployed in 6-8 weeks, but full cultural integration takes longer.

### 8. Do you offer training?
We design training *frameworks* and *leadership development programs*. Technical training is outsourced to subject matter experts.

### 9. Are your strategies compliant with the new labor law?
Absolutely. All our frameworks are built on the latest UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021.

### 10. Do you work with remote teams?
Yes, we specialize in designing frameworks for hybrid and remote workforce management.
