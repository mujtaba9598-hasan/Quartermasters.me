## FAQ

### 1. Do you handle permits?
Yes, we manage the entire permitting process with DTCM and other relevant authorities.

### 2. Can you suggest venues?
We have preferred partnerships with all major venues in the UAE and can secure competitive rates.

### 3. Do you provide security?
We design the security *plan* and contract licensed security firms for execution.

### 4. What happens if it rains?
We always have a contingency plan (Plan B) for outdoor events, including rapid-deploy structures.

### 5. Do you handle VIP protocol?
Yes, our team is trained in royal and diplomatic protocol to ensure seamless handling of dignitaries.

### 6. Can you manage ticketing?
Yes, we deploy secure digital ticketing and registration systems.

### 7. Do you have AV equipment?
We partner with top-tier technical suppliers to provide state-of-the-art audio-visual production.

### 8. How far in advance should we book?
For major events, 6-12 months is recommended. For smaller events, 3 months is sufficient.

### 9. Do you handle travel and accommodation?
Yes, our logistics team manages flights, hotels, and ground transport for delegates.

### 10. Can you brand the venue?
Absolutely. We handle all environmental branding, from screen content to large-format printing.
