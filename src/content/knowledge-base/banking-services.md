# Banking Services Consultancy

## Scope & Description
Strategic advisory on banking operations, regulatory compliance, capital structuring, and financial framework development for institutional and corporate clients. We navigate the complexities of the UAE financial ecosystem, ensuring robust compliance with Central Bank regulations and AFZA requirements.

### What We Do
- Banking Relationship Management & Account Structuring
- Financial Compliance Audits (AML/KYC)
- Capital Adequacy Stress Testing
- Treasury Operations Optimization
- Sovereign Wealth Fund Interface Strategy

### What We Don't Do
- Provide direct loans or financial guarantees
- Operate as a retail bank
- Facilitate unauthorized money transfers

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### 1. Can you guarantee a bank account opening?
No, we cannot guarantee approval as it is at the sole discretion of the bank's compliance committee. We guarantee a professionally structured application that maximizes success probability.

### 2. Do you handle personal banking?
We focus on corporate and institutional banking structures. Personal banking is only addressed for UBOs (Ultimate Beneficial Owners) in the context of corporate setup.

### 3. What is the typical timeframe for a corporate account?
It ranges from 4 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity of the ownership structure and the selected bank.

### 4. Do you provide tax advice?
We provide financial structural advice. For specific tax filings, we partner with specialized tax auditors.

### 5. Can you assist with crypto-related banking?
Yes, but this falls under our High-Risk Enhanced Due Diligence framework and requires specific regulatory approvals.

### 6. What documents are required for the initial assessment?
Company license (if existing), UBO passport copies, last 6 months' bank statements, and a business plan.

### 7. Do you work with offshore banks?
We primarily focus on UAE onshore and free zone banking but can advise on cross-border structures.

### 8. How do you charge?
We operate on a retainer or project fee basis. We do NOT take success fees or commissions from banks.

### 9. Are you a registered financial advisor?
We are a management consultancy licensed for banking services consultancy. We do not sell financial products.

### 10. What if our application is rejected?
We analyze the rejection reason, restructure the application if possible, or approach alternative banking partners.
