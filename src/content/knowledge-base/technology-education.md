# Technology Education & Research Consulting

## Scope & Description
Consulting for digital transformation, R&D strategy, and technology education infrastructure. We bridge the gap between academic research and commercial application, helping organizations build future-ready capabilities.

### What We Do
- Digital Transformation Roadmaps
- EdTech Infrastructure Design & Implementation
- Technology Scouting & Innovation Lab Setup
- Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Frameworks
- Cybersecurity Curriculum Development

### What We Don't Do
- Selling hardware (laptops, servers)
- Coding custom software (we design architecture, partner for dev)
- IT Support (Helpdesk)

## Service Tiers & Deliverables

### Express ($3,000 - $7,000)
- **Scope**: Technology audit or curriculum review.
- **Deliverables**: Technical Capability Assessment, Curriculum Gap Analysis.
- **Timeline**: 2-4 Weeks.

### Standard ($40,000 - $80,000)
- **Scope**: Digital transformation strategy or new program launch.
- **Deliverables**: Digital Roadmap (3-Year), Learning Management System (LMS) Architecture, faculty training plan.
- **Timeline**: 3-5 Months.

### Premium ($120,000 - $200,000)
- **Scope**: Innovation lab setup or campus-wide smart integration.
- **Deliverables**: Lab Blueprint, R&D Partnership Framework, Smart Campus IoT Strategy.
- **Timeline**: 6-9 Months.

### Enterprise ($250,000+)
- **Scope**: National education reform or large-scale R&D initiative.
- **Deliverables**: Sovereign Tech Roadmap, National Curriculum Framework, AI Strategy.
- **Timeline**: 12-24 Months.

## Process Timeline
1. **Discovery (Month 1-2)**: Assessing technological maturity and learning goals.
2. **Architecture (Month 3-5)**: Designing the technical and pedagogical framework.
3. **Integration (Month 6-9)**: Vendor selection, system deployment, and pilot testing.
4. **Scale (Month 10+)**: Full rollout and continuous improvement.

## FAQ

### 1. Do you build software?
We design the *architecture* and manage the development process with specialized vendors.

### 2. Can you help us get accredited?
We align curriculum with accreditation standards (ABET, CAA) but do not grant accreditation ourselves.

### 3. What technologies do you cover?
AI/ML, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, IoT, and Cloud Computing are our core focus areas.

### 4. Do you provide instructors?
We help recruit faculty or train existing staff (Train-the-Trainer models).

### 5. Can you set up a research lab?
Yes, we design the lab layout, equipment list, and research agenda.

### 6. How do you handle data privacy?
Data privacy is central to our designs, ensuring compliance with UAE and GDPR standards.

### 7. Do you sell LMS software?
We are platform-agnostic. We help you select and configure the best LMS for your needs (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, etc.).

### 8. What is "Technology Education Consulting"?
It is advising on *how* to teach technology effectively and *how* to use technology in education.

### 9. Can you help with digital exams?
Yes, we design secure digital assessment frameworks.

### 10. Do you work with schools (K-12)?
Primary focus is Higher Education and Corporate Training, but we can advise K-12 groups on strategy.
